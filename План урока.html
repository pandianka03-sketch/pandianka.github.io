2.1 Text for Reading: Astrology and Zodiac Signs

Astrology and Zodiac Signs

Astrology is the study of stars and planets. Many people believe that their zodiac sign can tell a lot about their personality. There are 12 zodiac signs. Each sign has different qualities.

For example, Aries is very confident and brave. They are sometimes impatient but always active. Taurus is patient and calm. They love comfort and are very reliable. Gemini is friendly and curious. They like talking and meeting new people.

People often say that Leo is the most confident sign, and Virgo is the most hardworking. Cancer is more emotional than other signs but also very caring.

Do you know your zodiac sign? What qualities do people say about it?

3. Grammar Focus: Adjectives and Their Degrees

We use adjectives to describe people. Let's learn about degrees of comparison Positive, comparative, superlative forms :

  • Positive: kind, strong, curious
  • Comparative: kinder, stronger, more curious
  • Superlative: the kindest, the strongest, the most curious

Example: "Leo is more confident than Cancer." / "Virgo is the most practical sign."
